Key Financial Calculations

Return on Equity (ROE)

$$ ROE = \frac{Net\ Income}{Shareholder\ Equity} \times 100 $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

$$ CAGR = \left(\frac{Ending\ Value}{Beginning\ Value}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 $$

Risk-Adjusted Return (Sharpe Ratio)

$$ Sharpe\ Ratio = \frac{R_p - R_f}{\sigma_p} $$

Where: $R_p$ = Portfolio Return, $R_f$ = Risk-free Rate, $\sigma_p$ = Standard Deviation

These formulas are fundamental to our financial analysis. ROE of 16.8% shows strong profitability. Our 5-year CAGR is 19.3%, and our Sharpe ratio of 1.8 indicates excellent risk-adjusted returns.

Transaction Analysis Query


SELECT 
    customer_segment,
    COUNT(*) as transaction_count,
    SUM(amount) as total_volume,
    AVG(amount) as avg_transaction_size,
    ROUND(SUM(amount) * 100.0 / 
          (SELECT SUM(amount) FROM transactions), 2) as pct_of_total
FROM transactions
WHERE transaction_date BETWEEN '2024-10-01' AND '2024-12-31'
    AND status = 'completed'
GROUP BY customer_segment
HAVING SUM(amount) > 1000000
ORDER BY total_volume DESC;

This query analyzes Q4 transaction patterns across customer segments

Our data warehouse processes over 50 million transactions per quarter. This SQL query segments customers by behavior and identifies high-value segments. Premium customers account for 42% of total volume.
